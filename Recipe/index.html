Delicious Vegan Cornbread

There is a vegan restaurant 5 mins away from my house. This restaurant serves the best vegan cornbread. It is delicious and moist. I wanted to find a similar recipe, so I could make it at home and save some money!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup yellow corn meal
  • 2/3 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon baking powder
  • 1 1/4 cup unsweetened almond milk
  • 1/3 cup canola oil
